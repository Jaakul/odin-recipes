Chicken Tikka Masala

This is like some curry stuff that they sautee some chicken in some oil infused with aromatics

Then a ton of spices are dumped in along with water and you let that stuff cook for a while

Then it thickens and gets all curry like

Ingredients list

  • boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Now that we have our ingredients, let's take a look at the steps needed to make this thiccc orange stuff

  1. put all your yogurt and spices and chicken in a bag or something
  2. marinate for 3 hrs
  3. heat up 1 tablespoon of oil and dump in chopped onions
  4. sautee until brown or something
  5. remove chicken from marinate and add to curry
  6. add more spices and add water
  7. cook for 30 min or until chicken is tender and curry is thiccc
  8. eat
